FEATURES

We are proud to introduce the new PSA Dagger Micro Pistol!

A slim-line-sized polymer-framed pistol, the Palmetto Dagger Micro with Extreme Carry Cuts introduces game-changing ergonomics and controllability. Made from high-quality stainless steel and advanced polymers, this pistol meets today's highest expectations while maintaining the unmatched Palmetto State Armory value. Chambered in 9x19mm, the Palmetto Dagger Micro is fed from a supplied PSA Dagger Micro Polymer (15-Round) Magazine and operated by a smooth single-action striker-fired trigger mechanism. The Palmetto Dagger Micro is feature-rich even in its standard form, yet it also provides a framework for further customization, as it is highly compatible with many aftermarket offerings. The Extreme Carry Cuts offer smoother edges to the rear and front of the slide to help with faster drawing without snagging the user's holster. The slide is also finished in Black Cerakote and Shield RMSc Optics Cut for compatibility with like-style reflex sights. Includes optics plate and screws.

FEATURES

The TASER Pulse 2 is a compact and durable self-defense tool built to keep protection within reach at all times. Constructed with a high-impact polymer housing, it offers exceptional strength while securing the internal components, all while maintaining a lightweight feel at just 8 ounces. Its textured grip ensures comfort and control during use, and the slim profile makes it easy to carry discreetly. Backed by the TASER Safe Escape Product Replacement Guarantee, you can deploy it in self-defense, leave it behind to get to safety, and Axon will replace the device free of charge under approved conditions.
